About Workplace Partners

✔   Our Mission and Values

Debbie Featherston founded Workplace Partners, LLC in 1997 with the goal of empowering people to take charge of their careers. Our mission is to inspire and support professional and organizational development, enabling leaders, managers, and employees to achieve their full potential, and organizations to attain their goals.

 

✔  Be Passionate

Developing people is our passion. The positive impact of our work on people’s lives, the workplace, and in organizations, inspires and motivates us to continue designing tailored solutions that deliver sustainable results.

 

✔  Keep Customers First

We are committed to our customers’ success. We build lasting high trust relationships with our clients and colleagues. Our motto is – Listen first, then listen more.

 

✔  Do the right thing

Acting with integrity, respect, and promoting diversity and inclusion gives our clients confidence in our team’s expertise, judgment, and intentions.

 

✔  Ignite team spirit

Workplace Partners’ team approach leads to innovative solutions uniquely tailored to your business needs. Our team personally grows and learns by sharing ideas and best practices that expand and challenge our understanding and capabilities. And yes, we play hard and have fun doing it!

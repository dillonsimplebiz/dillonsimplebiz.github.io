TAKE CHARGE OF YOUR CAREER

“ When we pursue a destiny that aligns with our life’s purpose, uses our gifts and talents, and involves that which we are most passionate about, then we are happiest and most productive.” Debbie Featherston


Chance and circumstance often drive our careers. Even though we know we should be intentional in creating a road map to guide our career, most of us don’t plan or strategize.  It just happens. If luck comes our way, we may find that we are using our basic talents. Many of us, while frustrated and disillusioned, fear making a change because we may lose money, security, or status. Others simply don’t know how to do it and are uncertain of the first step. Easily overwhelmed by the task, we toss in the towel before getting started.


Better opportunities are within reach.  Partnering with an experienced career coach will increase your success in resolving career issues, managing any transition, and growing your career.

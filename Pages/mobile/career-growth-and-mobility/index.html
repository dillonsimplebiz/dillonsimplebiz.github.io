Career Growth & Mobility

Consulting, Coaching & Training to Find Performance Solutions That Fit

Engage, Motivate, Retain & Empower Your Team

Become More Efficient

Many organizations are struggling to find effective recruitment, engagement, and retention solutions. Studies verify the unprecedented challenges involved in attracting and retaining top performing employees in the post-pandemic era.  


Workplace Partners helps organizations attract, develop, motivate, and retain its workforce. Our Career Growth and Mobility solutions support leadership development, management development, competency development, workforce planning, and team development. We offer consulting, training programs, and leadership and team coaching to help organizations build a culture of development, increase employee career satisfaction, and improve employee retention. 


Our approach involves you at every turn. Using a comprehensive approach, we gather and analyze the situation, design a tailored solution integrating assessments, training programs, and coaching, and then implement unique learning journeys for leaders and employees. 


Our proven approach to career development benefits employees, managers, and the organization. 

                                                                                                                        

Employees learn to better manage their own careers. Group workshops and career coaching help employees in making career decisions, discovering possibilities, and choosing career paths that align their career aspirations to the business needs. Career development strengthens the manager-employee relationship and encourages dialogue and feedback on employee performance and reputation, leading to actionable development plans.                           

Managers become talent builders. With heightened self confidence and skill in developing others, managers engage employees in regular career coaching conversations. As advocates and supporters of employee development, managers strategically invest in advancing, promoting, and developing top talent. They leverage insights about employee interests and career aspirations to better develop talent, match employees to roles, and plan promotions, resulting in retaining valued performers.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 

Organizations that integrate career development into their culture benefit from lower turnover, higher productivity, improved performance, and see a positive impact to their bottom-line.

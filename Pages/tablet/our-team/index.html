Our Team

Debbie Featherston

M.S.

Debbie Featherston is Founder and President of Workplace Partners, LLC. Her passion is working with leaders, teams, and individual contributors to build organizational talent and develop high trust relationships during restructurings, cultural transformations, and mergers and acquisitions. In collaboration with her team, she directs learning and development initiatives and specializes in leadership / management development, career management, human performance improvement, change agility, and resilience, resulting in maximizing workforce engagement and productivity. Working in 17 industries in over 30 countries, Debbie supports senior leaders to front line employees in organizations like BASF, Syngenta, Raytheon, Lucent Technologies, AT&T, MetLife, Boston Whaler, Teledyne, Technetics, United Space Alliance, NASA, among others.

Prior to Workplace Partners, Debbie was Vice President and co-founder of JIST Works, Inc based in Indianapolis, IN where she was a driving force in their revenue growth. Additionally, she designed and implemented systems and processes that provided a foundation to sustain fast growth for its national training and catalog sales.


Debbie has an M.S. in Adult Education from Indiana University and a B.A. in Psychology from Olivet University. She has written numerous articles and training manuals. Past president of Space Coast ASTD, Debbie is a certified Leadership Coach and certified facilitator of the MBTI, the Hogan Assessment, ATD’s Human Performance Improvement (HPI), and Covey’s Speed of Trust Simulation. 


Karl Lieb

M.A., Ph.D

Karl has over 30 years of leadership training and consultation experience working with Fortune 100 companies in the human and organizational performance improvement field. He has trained over 10,000 leaders and individual contributors at companies such as Chrysler, United Space Alliance, Wal Mart, Boeing, Rockwell International, among others. His experience includes consulting with companies in the areas of organizational change and leadership, human performance improvement (HPI), HPI needs assessment, providing one-on-one leadership coaching, designing and conducting management and executive teambuilding sessions, as well as developing and conducting leadership workshops. In addition to Karl’s consulting and training work with businesses, he has led major Organizational Development (OD) initiatives in forming High Performance Work Organizations.


Karl has taught Webster University and Florida Institute of Technology graduate courses. FIT undergraduate-level courses in Human Resources Management, Training and Development, Compensation and Benefits as well as Selection and Placement within the Human Resources Department. At Eastern Florida State College he teaches key HR courses in the BS program. Karl is a past member of the American Society for Training and Development (ASTD) and is the past president of the Space Coast Chapter, Society for Human Resources (SHRM), LEAD Brevard, The Greater Orlando Organizational Development (GOOD) Network, the HR Florida State Council and the International Listening Association.


Karl earned a Ph.D. in Leadership and Education with specialization in HRD from Barry University, an M.A. in Human Resources and an M.A. in Human Resources Development from Webster University, and a B.A. in Communications from The Ohio State University.

Patricia Hernández Curry

B.A.

Patricia has over 17 years of leadership experience working in the human performance improvement field. Her passion is providing coaching and training to empower leaders in a globalized world. Certified in Cultural Detective, the Conflict Dynamics Profile, MBTI, and Herrmann Brain Dominance Instrument (HBDI), she coaches and trains individuals in intercultural effectiveness, communicating across cultures, successfully adapting to different cultures, and adjusting to life in the USA. She coaches professionals from the US, Colombia, Spain, and Puerto Rico. Recently, Patricia facilitated leadership programs for managers and executives throughout Latin America. She has co-facilitated the multi-day Liderazgo Efectivo (Leadership Effectiveness Workshop) in Latin America to managers and executives from Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Venezuela, Panama, Costa Rica, Miami, and Mexico. Some corporate clients she works with include Ricoh, Unilever, Oracle, Sun Nuclear and the NGO CARE, Florida Department of Health, and University of Alabama’s executive education group. ï»¿

Patricia is a CTA Certified Coach approved by the International Coach Federation. She earned a B.A. in International Relations from Florida International University. As a lifelong learner, she completed graduate coursework in Foundations of Intercultural Communication, Cross-cultural Training in International Corporations, Teaching Intercultural Communications, and Training Design for Intercultural Learning in the MA Intercultural Relations program at the Intercultural Communications Institute and University of the Pacific, Portland, Oregon.

 

She speaks French and is bilingual English/Spanish. Born and raised in Bogotá, Colombia, she lives on Florida’s Space Coast. She is a member of the Society for Intercultural Education, Training and Research (SIETAR).

Curtis D. Curry

M.A., MBA, Ph.D

Curtis has provided training or coaching services to over 200 organizations from around the globe. For the past 20 years, he has dedicated his career to helping develop tens of thousands of global leaders. He has facilitated leadership development sessions for non-profit, government, and scores of private sector companies including Ricoh, Kraft, and the United Space Alliance (NASA’s largest subcontractor at the time). He speaks four languages, has published a number of book chapters on leadership development-related topics, and has an MBA, an M.A. in International Studies (focusing on international develoipment), and a Ph.D in leadership and HRD. He teaches leadership, communications, and people management at the MBA level and is a fellow at the Institute for Cross Cultural Management, Florida Tech. His leadership development practice focuses on managing change, global leadership development, and managing conflict effectively. He is a CTA Certified Executive Coach, Master Facilitator for HBDI and Whole Brain Thinking, Master Facilitator for Paradigm Learning (Financial Leadership), and has extensive experience using MBTI, Conflict Dynamics Profile, Change Style Indicator, FIRO-B, CCL’s Benchmarks and Skillscope, DiSC, and the Emotional Intelligence Appraisal among others.
